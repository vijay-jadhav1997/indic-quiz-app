🎯 Tips for smart Study 🎯

  1. Set Clear Goals:
    • Break down concepts into manageable goals.
    • Focus on the important & difficult topics first.
    • Allocate specific time for studying & stick to it.
  2. Active Learning:
    • Summarize, question, & explain topics in your own words
    • Diagrams, charts, & mind maps can simplify complex topics.
    • Regularly test yourself.
  3. Stay Healthy:
    • Sleep for 7-8 hrs to keep your mind sharp.
    • Regular physical activity, Yoga & meditation boosts focus & memory.
  4. Stay Positive & Persistent:
    • Review frequently to reinforce memory.
    • Study in a distraction-free zone.
